3S Battery Management Unit for MRI-Compatible Display System

A custom-designed 3S battery management system for powering a tablet in an MRI environment. The design focuses on safety, reliability, and thermal management.

1. Component Selection: Boost Converter to Linear Regulator (LM7805)

Transition from boost converter (inductor hazard in MRI) to linear regulator: sacrificing efficiency for safety, removing magnetic components from LM7805 module, and designing custom heatsink for 3-hour continuous operation.

2. 3-Cell Protection Circuit

Microcontroller/Processor Selection

Selection and configuration of the 5V microcontroller unit for system control.

3. Custom Thermal Management with Aluminum Heatsink

The aluminum heatsink was designed to dissipate heat from the linear regulator, ensuring safe operation.

4. Thermal Camera Testing: With and Without Heatsink

Thermal imaging shows a significant temperature reduction when using the custom heatsink.

Note: Trade-offs considered between MRI safety requirements, thermal performance, and cost efficiency.

注：所有设计均在特定约束条件下优化（MRI安全、热管理、成本）

LED Light Driver - KiCAD Design

A complete schematic and PCB design for an LED light driver, created in KiCAD. The design includes component selection and layout for optimal performance.

Schematic Design

The schematic for the LED driver circuit for a desktop lamp.

PCB Layout

PCB editor view of the circuit. Components are placed within close proximity of their nearest connection to reduce track length. Bottom track layer is used to avoid interference.

Note on Prototype: Due to time constraints in this academic exercise, the design was validated through simulation and review. The focus was on mastering the complete schematic capture to PCB layout workflow in KiCAD, including design rule checking (DRC) and generating manufacturing files (Gerbers).
